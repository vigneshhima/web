The Internet of Things (IoT) is a fascinating and rapidly evolving field that connects physical devices to the internet, enabling them to collect and share data. These devices, often referred to as "smart objects," can range from simple home appliances like smart thermostats to complex industrial machinery and transportation systems
IoT devices are embedded with sensors, software, and network connectivity, allowing them to communicate with each other and other internet-enabled devices. This creates a vast network of interconnected devices that can exchange data and perform various tasks autonomously
